Our Story

We Believe Every Book
Changes a Life

Founded in 2026 by a group of passionate book lovers in ISTAD, Bookery was built on one simple idea, everyone deserves access to great books at fair prices.

How It All Started

It started in a class with five friends, hundreds of books, and a shared dream. We noticed that books in Cambodia were hard to find, overpriced, or simply unavailable in the genres readers wanted.

We decided to do something about it. In 2026, Bookery was launched as an online bookstore focused on providing an incredible selection of books at prices that wouldn't break the bank, delivered fast and packaged with care.

Today, we're proud to serve over 5000 readers across Cambodia and the region. We've grown from a startup into a community of book lovers who support and inspire each other every day.

What Drives Us

Our Mission

To make great books accessible to everyone, regardless of where they live or what they can afford. We believe reading changes lives, and we're committed to removing every barrier that stands between a reader and their next great book.

Our Vision

To become Cambodia's most beloved bookstore, a place where every reader feels at home, every author feels celebrated, and every book finds its reader. We envision a future where reading is a daily habit for millions across the region.
